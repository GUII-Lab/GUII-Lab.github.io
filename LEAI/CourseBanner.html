Course Banner

One notification banner for the whole course. When enabled, every student sees it on their survey — general, in-group, and structured reflection alike.

Show the banner to students Off by default. When off, students see no banner.

Keep it short — one or two sentences read best. Longer text wraps and can crowd the chat.

Behavior

Audience

Show to a random subset (A/B) Off shows the banner to everyone. On shows it to a random share of students; the rest are a silent control group.

Assignment is anonymous and stays the same for a student across reloads. You can see exactly who saw the banner in the Feedback Analyzer response list.
