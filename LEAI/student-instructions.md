# LEAI Reflection — Student Instructions

## Individual Reflection

Open the reflection link: https://guii-lab.github.io/LEAI/feedback.html?id=OhfYteZ46xFQ

On the "Before you begin" screen, check the required box (the optional research box is up to you), then click **Continue**. The chatbot will walk you through the reflection one section at a time — type your answers in the chat box or use the mic icon to dictate, and respond to any follow-ups it asks. When you've covered every section, the bot will tell you the reflection is complete and a **"Download my reflection (Word / .docx)"** button will appear at the bottom of the chat. Click it, save the file to your computer, and upload that `.docx` to this Canvas assignment to submit.

> You can keep chatting after the button appears if you want to revise something — just click Download again before submitting.
